Our Mission

Code Should Have No Borders

ProtoSpark was founded by high school students who believe every child, no matter their country, language, or background, deserves access to quality computer science education. We host free virtual coding camps that build critical thinking, creativity, and real-world problem-solving skills. Every camper leaves with something they're proud of.

🎯

Free & Accessible

100% free for students. We work to translate curriculum into multiple languages so no child is left behind.

🌐

International Reach

We organize chapters country-by-country, led by local volunteer leaders who bridge language and time-zone gaps.

🚀

Student-Led

Founded and run entirely by high schoolers who are passionate about giving back and building real leadership experience.

🔮

Future-Ready Skills

We teach the thinking, not just the tool. Students build problem-solving instincts and logical reasoning that translate far beyond coding — and leave with a real project to prove it.
