Leadership

Founders & Directors

Ananya Karthick

Co-Founder & Executive Director

Ananya is a sophomore in the IB program at J. R. Tucker High School, passionate about leadership and social impact. She co-founded ProtoSpark to ensure every child, no matter where they grow up, has equal access to quality CS education.

Akshith Nair

Co-Founder & Executive Director

Akshith is a sophomore at Deep Run High School's Center for Information Technology, passionate about computer science and real-world impact. He co-founded ProtoSpark to empower kids worldwide through accessible, engaging technology education.

Harshika Thouti

Co-Founder & Executive Director

Harshika is a sophomore at Deep Run High School's Center for Information Technology, passionate about innovation and entrepreneurship. She co-founded ProtoSpark to make STEM more accessible and engaging for kids around the world.
